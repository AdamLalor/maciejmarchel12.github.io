About Malta

Malta is a Mediterranean island off the coast of Tunisia and to the south of Sicily, Malta has a Mediterranean climate, it has very mild winters and warm to hot summers. Rain occurs mainly in winter, with summer being generally dry but thunder storms can occur during the summer. Malta's languages consit of Maltese and English
